What do we do?

At Mecena, we strive to deliver top quality products and content for our clients, on a vast range of services, in order to save precious time to our clients.

At a fraction of the usual costs at a professional level, Mecena delivers tailored products such as audio works or graphic designs.

We work, you create.

Solution

Named after the latin art patron “Maecenas”, who spent his life scouting and supporting the best artists of the ancient Roman Empire; our company mission, draws inspiration from his role in world of arts, and the possibility of making creativity a more accessible process.

We always aspired to create a platform that could present and deliver the answer to most of these issues, and this is why we created Mecena.

Problem

From producing itself, mixing and mastering to the creation of an appropriate design or logo to represent your work; today the range of expertise required to complete a project is quite broad.
